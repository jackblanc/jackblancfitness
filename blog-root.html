Intermittent Fasting: The most powerful tool for your diet

Intermittent Fasting is an eating pattern in which you consume all of your calories during an eight-hour window each day, and remain in a fasted state throughout the remaining sixteen hours. Most people choose to skip breakfast, eating during a window of roughly noon until 8 PM. Intermittent fasting can have profound health benefits and makes dieting effortless, and I encourage you to try it. Read this article for more information.
